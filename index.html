ABOUT

Reflechir is the annual National level technical symposium of the Electronics and communication Engineering of K.L.N. College of Engineering. Reflechir was first incepted in the 2000s as a humble affair, by our students and faculties. Recent editions have seen the event grow exponentially, with the latest edition bearing witness to this fact, recording an unheralded footfall of over a quarter thousand participants. Conveniently scheduled on the more relaxed days of a sapping, short and sweet Even Semester - FEBRUARY 26, REFLECHIR’15 provides the perfect platform to showcase technical acumen, compete with peers and most important of all, imbibe inspiration.

PAPER PRESENTATION

All the presenters bring to their role something different than what the author put on paper. The presenters are requested to present their papers with only occasional reference to their notes rather than by reading out previously prepared texts.


SUGGESTED DOMAINS
  • Green Electronics
  • Nanotechnology/Spintronics
  • Fuzzy Logic
  • RF and Microwave/MEMS
  • Signal/Image Processing
  • VLSI/Embedded/Robotics
  • Networking
  • Wireless Communication/Optical communication
  • Other innovative topics

RULES & REGULATIONS
  • Maximum of 2 authors shall present a paper.
  • The registration fee is Rs. 100/- (either by DD or Spot Payment) per author.
  • There is no on spot registration.
  • Demand Draft should be taken in favor of ‘THE PRINCIPAL, K.L.N COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING’ (Payable at Madurai).
  • Full paper should be sent to reflechirece15@gmail.com with the subject of the mail as Paper presentation.
  • The paper should be in IEEE format.
  • The front page should have title of paper on the top and the details of the team members including mail id and contact numbers at the bottom.

CIRCUIT/PROGRAM DEBUGGING


The activity of "debugging", or removing bugs, ends when people get tired of doing it, not when the bugs are removed."This event comprises of circuit as well as program debugging. The participants can choose any stream of their interest. For example, if they choose circuit debugging, they may proceed with quiz on circuits as their first round and then circuit debugging as their second round and vice-versa for program debugging.


PHASE 1 - QUIZ

Technical Quiz on Electronic circuits/programming (participants can choose any one).

PHASE 2 - DEBUGGING

 Circuit/program debugging. It is a debugging round in which the teams have to find out the bugs in the circuit/program given to them and debug them and make the circuit/program work.


RULES & REGULATIONS
